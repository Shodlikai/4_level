A Guide to South American Cuisine

South America is a continent made out of 12 different countries, famous for football, carnivals, numerous tourist attractions and delicious food. With their rich history and diverse populations, South American countries offer delicious traditional dishes suited for everyone's taste and budget.

A Brief History of South American Food

Corn and potato are the staple of native South American food. Many South Americans make fresh cornbread and corn tortillas (thin flatbread) every day. When it comes to potatoes, chuño is definitely worth mentioning. Chuño is a freeze-dried potato product which originates before the 13th century. Due to its nutritional value and portability, chuño was a favorite dish of South American soldiers.

When the first Europeans arrived to South America, they introduced new foods. In particular, they brought animals that were not native to South America, like pigs, chickens, cows and goats. South Americans also adopted wheat and almonds from the Europeans, while the Europeans brought potato and corn back to Europe.

European occupying powers also shipped slaves from Africa. Over the years, African populations also made an impact on South American cuisine. For example, Menudo, a traditional soup, derived from the Spaniards' habit of giving cow's intestines (stomach) to their slaves, so the slaves learned how to season it well and make it tasty.

Today, foods such as quinoa or chia seeds, originating from South America, are universally accepted as super foods, popular for their great nutritional value.

Soups and Stews

Sopa de mondongo is one of the soups that are eaten across the continent. There are many variations of this slow-cooked tripe (cow's or pig's stomach) and vegetables soup. While in Colombia this soup is considered to be an addition to a larger meal, the Venezuelans see this soup as being too heavy for the stomach, so they eat it as the main course.

Sea food is eaten all over South America. In Brazil, fish stew called Moqueca is particularly popular. This dish is made with salt water fish, and optionally served with prawns. Records show that people in Brazil have been making this dish for at least 300 years.

Fun fact: Paraguayan dish called sopa paraguaya (Paraguyan soup) is actually not a soup, but a spongy corn cake, made with cheese and milk.

Main Course Meat Dishes

Originating from Peru, ceviche is one of the dishes whose popularity has spread across the world. This very unique dish is typically made from fresh raw fish cured in lemon or lime juice. Although it is delicious, you must be very careful with this dish, because not preparing and consuming it fresh could lead to food poisoning.

Churrasco refers to beef or, more generally, grilled meat, sometimes also called asado in countries such as Uruguay. In Chile, churrasco sandwich is accompanied with tomato, avocado and mayonnaise, while churrasco a lo pobre (poor man's churrasco) is eaten with French fries, fried egg and onions. Churrascarias are popular fast food restaurants which serve all-you-can-eat grilled meat for a fixed price.

Desserts

South Americans love desserts. In Brazil, the most popular ones are brigadeiros. This dessert can come in different colors and flavors, but the base is always the same: sweetened condensed milk is heated with cocoa powder until they form a paste, which is then rolled into small balls, and covered with toppings such as sprinkles or coconut shavings. Brigadeiros are often served at birthday parties.

Picarones, originating from Peru, are one of the oldest desserts, dating back to Peru's colonial days. Since there are more than 3,800 types of potatoes growing in Peru, desserts are often made with potatoes. Sweet potato, mixed with squash, is used for picarones. In combination with flour, sweet potatoes are turned into light and airy deep-fried doughnuts and served with syrup.

Fun fact: Wheat did not grow in South America, so white flour was very expensive. Two Venezuelan sisters invented a cake that did not need flour, but was based on mashed plantains (bananas) and corn instead. The cake, called Torta Bejarana, is still very popular, normally showered with sesame seeds.

Janubiy Amerika Oshxonasi Bo'yicha Qo'llanma

Janubiy Amerika — 12 xil davlatdan tashkil topgan qit'a bo'lib, futbol, karnavallar, ko'plab sayyohlik maskanlari va mazali taomlari bilan mashhur. Boy tarixi va xilma-xil aholisi bilan Janubiy Amerika mamlakatlari har kimning ta'bi va hamyoniga mos keladigan mazali an'anaviy taomlarni taklif etadi.

Janubiy Amerika Taomlarining Qisqacha Tarixi

Makka jo'xori va kartoshka mahalliy Janubiy Amerika taomlarining asosiy mahsulotidir. Ko'plab janubiy amerikaliklar har kuni yangi makkajo'xori noni va makkajo'xori tortilyalari (yupqa non) tayyorlaydilar. Kartoshka haqida gap ketganda, chuñoni alohida ta'kidlash joiz. Chuño — 13-asrdan oldin paydo bo'lgan muzlatib quritilgan kartoshka mahsuloti. Oziqaviy qiymati va olib yurishga qulayligi tufayli chuño Janubiy Amerika askarlarining sevimli taomi bo'lgan.

Birinchi yevropaliklar Janubiy Amerikaga kelganda, ular yangi oziq-ovqatlarni olib kirishdi. Xususan, ular Janubiy Amerikada bo'lmagan cho'chqalar, tovuqlar, sigirlar va echkilar kabi hayvonlarni olib kelishdi. Janubiy amerikaliklar yevropaliklardan bug'doy va bodomni o'zlashtirdilar, yevropaliklar esa kartoshka va makkajo'xorini Yevropaga olib ketishdi.

Yevropa bosqinchi kuchlari Afrikadan qullarni ham olib kelishgan. Yillar davomida Afrika aholisi ham Janubiy Amerika oshxonasiga ta'sir o'tkazdi. Masalan, an'anaviy sho'rva bo'lgan Menudo ispanlarning qullarga sigir ichak-chavoqlarini (oshqozonini) berish odatidan kelib chiqqan, shuning uchun qullar uni qanday qilib yaxshi ziravorlashni va mazali qilishni o'rganib olishgan.

Bugungi kunda Janubiy Amerikadan kelib chiqqan kinoa yoki chia urug'lari kabi oziq-ovqatlar butun dunyoda "super taomlar" (super foods) sifatida qabul qilinib, yuqori oziqaviy qiymati bilan mashhurdir.

Sho'rvalar va Qaynatmalar (Stews)

Sopa de mondongo — butun qit'a bo'ylab iste'mol qilinadigan sho'rvalardan biri. Bu sekin pishiriladigan qorinch (sigir yoki cho'chqa oshqozoni) va sabzavotli sho'rvaning ko'plab turlari mavjud. Kolumbiyada bu sho'rva kattaroq ovqatga qo'shimcha deb hisoblansa, venesuelaliklar bu sho'rvani oshqozon uchun juda og'ir deb bilishadi, shuning uchun ular uni asosiy taom sifatida iste'mol qilishadi.

Dengiz mahsulotlari butun Janubiy Amerika bo'ylab iste'mol qilinadi. Braziliyada Moqueca deb ataladigan baliqli ragu (qaynatma) ayniqsa mashhur. Bu taom sho'r suv baliqlaridan tayyorlanadi va ixtiyoriy ravishda krevetkalar (prawns) bilan tortiladi. Ma'lumotlar shuni ko'rsatadiki, Braziliyadagi odamlar bu taomni kamida 300 yildan beri tayyorlab kelishmoqda.

Qiziqarli fakt: Sopa paraguaya (Paragvay sho'rvasi) deb ataladigan Paragvay taomi aslida sho'rva emas, balki pishloq va sut qo'shilgan g'ovak makkajo'xori pirogidir.

Asosiy Go'shtli Taomlar

Perudan kelib chiqqan seviche (ceviche) mashhurligi butun dunyoga tarqalgan taomlardan biridir. Bu juda o'ziga xos taom odatda limon yoki laym sharbatida "pishirilgan" (cured) yangi xom baliqdan tayyorlanadi. Garchi u mazali bo'lsa-da, siz bu taom bilan juda ehtiyot bo'lishingiz kerak, chunki uni yangi holda tayyorlamaslik va iste'mol qilmaslik oziq-ovqatdan zaharlanishga olib kelishi mumkin.

Churrasco mol go'shti yoki umuman olganda, panjarada pishirilgan go'shtni anglatadi, Urugvay kabi mamlakatlarda ba'zan asado deb ham ataladi. Chilida churrasco sendvichi pomidor, avokado va mayonez bilan birga tortiladi, churrasco a lo pobre (kambag'al odam churrascosi) esa qovurilgan kartoshka (fri), qovurilgan tuxum va piyoz bilan iste'mol qilinadi. Churrascarias — bu belgilangan narx evaziga xohlagancha panjarada pishirilgan go'sht tortiladigan mashhur tez ovqatlanish restoranlari.

Shirinliklar

Janubiy amerikaliklar shirinliklarni yaxshi ko'radilar. Braziliyada eng mashhurlari bu brigadeirosdir. Bu shirinlik turli xil ranglar va ta'mlarda bo'lishi mumkin, ammo asosi har doim bir xil: shirin quyultirilgan sut kakao kukuni bilan pasta hosil bo'lguncha qizdiriladi, keyin kichik sharchalar shaklida dumaloqlanadi va ustiga qandolat bezaklari yoki kokos qirindisi sepiladi. Brigadeiros ko'pincha tug'ilgan kunlarda tortiladi.

Perudan kelib chiqqan Picarones eng qadimgi shirinliklardan biri bo'lib, Peruning mustamlaka davriga borib taqaladi. Peruda 3800 dan ortiq turdagi kartoshka o'sganligi sababli, shirinliklar ko'pincha kartoshkadan tayyorlanadi. Picarones uchun qovoq bilan aralashtirilgan shirin kartoshka ishlatiladi. Un bilan aralashtirilib, shirin kartoshka yengil va havodor, yog'da qovurilgan ponchiklar (doughnuts)ga aylantiriladi va qiyom (sirop) bilan tortiladi.

Qiziqarli fakt: Janubiy Amerikada bug'doy o'smagan, shuning uchun oq un juda qimmat edi. Ikki venesuelalik opa-singil un talab qilmaydigan, balki uning o'rniga ezilgan plantayn (banan turi) va makkajo'xoriga asoslangan pirog ixtiro qilishdi. Torta Bejarana deb ataladigan bu pirog hali ham juda mashhur bo'lib, odatda ustiga kunjut urug'lari sepiladi.
