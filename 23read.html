New York couple find safe containing $100k in lake

James Kane and Barbie Agostini, a couple from New York City, were trying out a hobby called “magnet fishing” at a lake in Flushing Meadows Corona Park. They threw a line with a strong magnet into the water on a Friday afternoon in May. To their surprise, they pulled out a safe containing $100,000 cash. Despite the money being damaged by the water, they were excited about their unexpected discovery.

In an interview, James Kane explained that they started magnet fishing during the Covid-19 pandemic because it was a cheap way to go treasure-hunting. Magnet fishing involves using a rope with a strong magnet to find metal objects in water. Finding a safe filled with cash was beyond their expectations.

The couple contacted the New York police department about their find and were told they could keep the money because there was no way to identify the original owner of the safe. Barbie Agostini was amazed by their luck, saying she couldn’t believe it.

This was the most extraordinary find for the couple, who had previously discovered old guns, World War II grenades, a motorcycle, foreign coins, and jewellery.

How Rain Can Make You Happier and Healthier

Rainy days might seem gloomy, but they actually have surprising benefits for our well-being. Scientists have found that rain can boost our happiness and mental health.

In the UK, where rain is common, people experience an average of 159 rainy days each year. Despite the tendency to stay indoors when it rains, there are compelling reasons to venture out into the rain. Walking in the rain or after it has stopped can be beneficial for both our bodies and minds. Scientists believe that negative ions play a role in this. These ions, present in rain and other natural settings like waterfalls and the sea, could enhance our mood, reduce stress, and increase our energy levels.

Additionally, the air becomes cleaner after rainfall, and the earthy scent called petrichor, created by bacteria in soil, can have a calming effect when inhaled. Going outdoors in the rain offers various benefits, including relaxation, physical activity, and social interaction.

Regardless of age, spending time in nature is important for our well-being. So, next time it rains, don’t hesitate to put on your raincoat and enjoy the outdoors.

Nyu-Yorklik juftlik ko'lda ichida 100 ming dollar bo'lgan seyf topib oldi

Jeyms Keyn va Barbi Agostini, Nyu-York shahridan bo'lgan juftlik, Flushing Meadows Corona parkidagi ko'lda "magnitli baliq ovi" deb nomlangan xobbini sinab ko'rishayotgan edi. Ular may oyidagi juma kunining tushdan keyingi vaqtida suvga kuchli magnitli arqonni tashladilar. Ularning hayratiga, ular ichida 100 000 dollar naqd pul bo'lgan seyfni tortib olishdi. Pul suvdan zararlanganiga qaramay, ular o'zlarining kutilmagan kashfiyotlaridan hayajonda edilar.

Intervyuda, Jeyms Keyn Covid-19 pandemiyasi davrida magnitli baliq ovini boshlaganliklarini tushuntirdi, chunki bu xazina izlashga borishning arzon usuli edi. Magnitli baliq ovi suvda metall obyektlarni topish uchun kuchli magnitli arqondan foydalanishni o'z ichiga oladi. Naqd pul bilan to'ldirilgan seyfni topish ularning kutganlaridan ham ziyod edi.

Juftlik o'zlarining topilmalari haqida Nyu-York politsiya departamenti bilan bog'lanishdi va ularga pulni o'zlarida olib qolishlari mumkinligini aytishdi, chunki seyfning asl egasini aniqlashning iloji yo'q edi. Barbi Agostini ularning omadidan hayratda qolib, bunga ishona olmasligini aytdi.

Bu avvalroq eski qurollar, Ikkinchi Jahon urushi granatalari, mototsikl, xorijiy tangalar va zargarlik buyumlarini kashf etgan juftlik uchun eng g'ayrioddiy topilma bo'ldi.

Qanday qilib yomg'ir sizni baxtliroq va sog'lomroq qilishi mumkin

Yomg'irli kunlar g'amgin tuyulishi mumkin, ammo ular aslida bizning farovonligimiz uchun hayratlanarli foydalarga ega. Olimlar yomg'ir bizning baxtimizni va ruhiy salomatligimizni oshirishi mumkinligini aniqladilar.

Yomg'ir odatiy hol bo'lgan Buyuk Britaniyada, odamlar har yili o'rtacha 159 yomg'irli kunni boshdan kechirishadi. Yomg'ir yoqqanda bino ichida qolish moyilligiga qaramay, yomg'ir ostiga chiqish uchun jiddiy sabablar bor. Yomg'irda yoki u to'xtaganidan keyin sayr qilish ham tanamiz, ham ongimiz uchun foydali bo'lishi mumkin. Olimlar salbiy ionlar bunda rol o'ynaydi deb hisoblashadi. Yomg'irda va sharsharalar hamda dengiz kabi boshqa tabiiy muhitlarda mavjud bo'lgan bu ionlar kayfiyatimizni yaxshilashi, stressni kamaytirishi va energiya darajamizni oshirishi mumkin.

Bundan tashqari, havo yomg'irdan keyin tozaroq bo'ladi va tuproqdagi bakteriyalar tomonidan yaratilgan petrikor deb ataladigan tuproq hidi, nafas olganda tinchlantiruvchi ta'sirga ega bo'lishi mumkin. Yomg'irda ochiq havoga chiqish turli xil foydalarni, jumladan dam olish, jismoniy faollik va ijtimoiy o'zaro aloqani taklif qiladi.

Yoshdan qat'i nazar, tabiatda vaqt o'tkazish bizning farovonligimiz uchun muhimdir. Shunday qilib, keyingi marta yomg'ir yoqqanda, yomg'irpo'shingizni kiyishdan va ochiq havodan bahramand bo'lishdan tortinmang.
