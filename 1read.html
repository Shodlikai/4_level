Beautiful Natural Places

Guess Where?

I

This 100 million year-old rock formation extends to 3,000 feet (900 meters) above the ground. It is located in a mountainous area, yet not too far away from the ocean. Where do you think that could be?

El Capitan, Yosemite National Park, the United States of America

The El Capitan cliff is found on the north side of Yosemite Valley in California. The name, given to it in 1851, means "the chief" in Spanish. El Cap, as it is popularly called, attracts many rock climbers who feel challenged by how flat the cliff is.

For the longest time, people thought El Cap was impossible to climb until three climbers managed to do it in 1958. They used special tactics to climb the rock in 47 days. Things changed since then because in 2017, Alex Honnold completed the first ever ascent of El Capitan without any protective equipment in only 4 hours.

II

These ruins of an ancient city are located in a very green and hilly area. Lovely animals called llamas like to visit this place. Could it be somewhere in the Americas, or is it in Europe?

Machu Picchu, Peru

Lying at 8,000 feet (2,430 meters) above the ground, Machu Picchu is known as The Lost City of the Incas, the indigenous people of South America. Archaeologists believe that the Incas started building this glorious city around 1430, but completely abandoned it once European conquerors arrived. No more than 750 people lived in Machu Picchu.

Because of the specific area where it was built, the city is surrounded by the most unique animals and plants, including butterflies which do not live anywhere else in the world.

III

This small waterfall stands close to a mountain, in an uninhabited area. The sky above them changes its blue color to green and red when the night falls. Do you know where this phenomenon happens?

Kirkjufellsfoss Waterfall, Iceland

This stunning waterfall, which channels the water from one of the many glaciers in Iceland, stands next to the Kirkjufell Mountain (Church Mountain in Icelandic). This isolated mountain is believed to be the most photographed place in Iceland, due to its remarkable nature. The area around the waterfall is one of the few places in the world where you can see aurora borealis, better known as northern lights.

These green, purple and red lights occasionally start dancing across the dark winter sky, making the whole landscape even more beautiful.

IV

Imagine a place with golden fields where you can smell fresh air and see low green hills everywhere around you. If you knew this place was also known for its wines and art, could you guess what it is?

Tuscany, Italy

Olive trees, sunflower fields, farms and vineyards spread across Tuscany, a region in central Italy. What's perhaps even more enchanting are its ancient castles, which look like they have been untouched for centuries.

The land in Tuscany is relatively low, with occasional hills and mountains. The region got its name from the Etruscan people who settled there around 3,000 years ago. There are over 120 protected nature reserves, which proves how valuable this dream-like region is.

V

This long, curvy river is surrounded by green hills and tall rocks. It is found in a place with many plant and animal species, and known for its good weather. The country where this place is located is at the same time the edge of a continent.

Blyde River Canyon, South Africa

The Blyde River formed this 16 mile (25 km)-long and 2,461 feet (750 meter)-deep wonder of nature, one of the largest canyons in the world. In Dutch, blyde means "happy, glad", and this name was given to the river by Dutch explorers in 1844.

The Blyde River Canyon is certainly one of the greatest places in the world, due to the subtropical climate in the area. The wildlife in the canyon is so diverse – there are monkeys and antelopes, as well as some dangerous animals like hippos and crocodiles.

VI

There is a beautiful rock formation in the shape of an arch surrounded by the sea. You can explore the area around it on a boat, swim in the warm waters, or sunbathe on the beach. Where could this perfect holiday destination be?

Cathedral Cove, New Zealand

One of the most visited tourist attractions in New Zealand, the Coromandel Peninsula offers more than 250 miles (400km) of white sand beaches. The best way to tour the peninsula is in a kayak – you can go around the entire coast, or even descend down a waterfall.

Even though not many people live on the Coromandel nowadays, it is believed to be one of the earliest Polynesian settlements. It is a popular retirement destination – nearly a third of all residents on the peninsula are aged 65 or over. Cathedral Cove, named after the large arch located there, is a marine reserve, reachable only by boat or on foot.

Go'zal Tabiiy Joylar

Toping-chi qayerda?

I

Ushbu 100 million yoshli qoya hosilasi yerdan 3000 fut (900 metr) balandlikkacha cho'zilgan. U tog'li hududda joylashgan, ammo okeandan juda uzoq emas. Sizningcha, bu qayerda bo'lishi mumkin?

El Kapitan, Yosemit milliy bog'i, Amerika Qo'shma Shtatlari

El Kapitan qoyasi Kaliforniyadagi Yosemit vodiysining shimoliy tomonida joylashgan. 1851-yilda berilgan bu nom ispanchada "boshliq" degan ma'noni anglatadi. Xalq orasida "El Kap" deb ataladigan bu joy, qoyaning tekisligidan hayratda qolgan ko'plab alpinistlarni o'ziga jalb qiladi.

Uzoq vaqt davomida odamlar 1958-yilda uchta alpinist unga chiqishga muvaffaq bo'lmaguncha, El Kapga chiqishning iloji yo'q deb o'ylashgan. Ular qoyaga 47 kun ichida chiqish uchun maxsus usullardan foydalanishgan. O'shandan beri vaziyat o'zgardi, chunki 2017-yilda Aleks Honnold hech qanday himoya vositalarisiz atigi 4 soat ichida El Kapitanga birinchi marta ko'tarilishni yakunladi.

II

Qadimgi shaharning bu xarobalari juda yashil va tepalikli hududda joylashgan. Llamalar deb ataladigan yoqimli hayvonlar bu joyga tashrif buyurishni yaxshi ko'radilar. Bu Amerika qit'asida bo'lishi mumkinmi yoki Yevropadami?

Machu Pikchu, Peru

Yerdan 8000 fut (2430 metr) balandlikda joylashgan Machu Pikchu Janubiy Amerikaning tub aholisi bo'lgan inkalarning "Yo'qolgan shahri" sifatida tanilgan. Arxeologlar inkalar ushbu ulug'vor shaharni 1430-yillarda qurishni boshlagan deb hisoblashadi, ammo yevropalik bosqinchilar kelganidan keyin uni butunlay tark etishgan. Machu Pikchuda 750 tadan ko'p bo'lmagan odam yashagan.

Shahar qurilgan o'ziga xos hudud tufayli u eng noyob hayvonlar va o'simliklar, jumladan dunyoning boshqa hech bir joyida yashamaydigan kapalaklar bilan o'ralgan.

III

Bu kichik sharshara tog'ga yaqin, aholi yashamaydigan hududda joylashgan. Kecha tushganda uning ustidagi osmon o'zining ko'k rangini yashil va qizilga o'zgartiradi. Bu hodisa qayerda sodir bo'lishini bilasizmi?

Kirkjufellsfoss Sharsharasi, Islandiya

Islandiyadagi ko'plab muzliklarning biridan suv olib keladigan ushbu ajoyib sharshara Kirkjufell tog'i (island tilida Cherkov tog'i) yonida joylashgan. Bu alohida tog' o'zining ajoyib tabiati tufayli Islandiyada eng ko'p suratga olingan joy hisoblanadi. Sharshara atrofidagi hudud dunyoda shimoliy yog'du (Aurora Borealis)ni ko'rish mumkin bo'lgan kam sonli joylardan biridir.

Ushbu yashil, binafsha va qizil chiroqlar vaqti-vaqti bilan qishki qorong'u osmonda raqsga tushib, butun manzarani yanada go'zal qiladi.

IV

Oltin dalalari bo'lgan, toza havodan nafas oladigan va atrofingizda past yashil tepaliklarni ko'radigan joyni tasavvur qiling. Agar bu joy o'zining vinolari va san'ati bilan ham mashhurligini bilsangiz, bu nima ekanligini topa olasizmi?

Tuskana, Italiya

Zaytun daraxtlari, kungaboqar dalalari, fermalar va uzumzorlar markaziy Italiyadagi Tuskana bo'ylab tarqalgan. Ehtimol, yanada maftunkor tomoni uning qadimiy qal'alari bo'lib, ular asrlar davomida tegishilmagandek ko'rinadi.

Tuskanadagi yer nisbatan past bo'lib, vaqti-vaqti bilan tepaliklar va tog'lar uchraydi. Viloyat o'z nomini 3000 yil oldin u yerga joylashgan etrusk xalqidan olgan. U yerda 120 dan ortiq himoyalangan tabiat qo'riqxonalari bor, bu esa ushbu tushdagidek mintaqaning qanchalik qadrli ekanligini isbotlaydi.

V

Bu uzun, egri-bugri daryo yashil tepaliklar va baland qoyalar bilan o'ralgan. U ko'plab o'simlik va hayvon turlari mavjud bo'lgan va yaxshi ob-havosi bilan tanilgan joyda topilgan. Bu joy joylashgan mamlakat ayni paytda qit'aning chekkasidir.

Blyde River Kanyoni, Janubiy Afrika

Blyde daryosi 16 mil (25 km) uzunlikdagi va 2461 fut (750 metr) chuqurlikdagi ushbu tabiat mo'jizasini, dunyodagi eng katta kanyonlardan birini hosil qilgan. Golland tilida "blyde" "baxtli, xursand" degan ma'noni anglatadi va bu nom daryoga 1844-yilda gollandiyalik tadqiqotchilar tomonidan berilgan.

Blyde River Kanyoni hududdagi subtropik iqlim tufayli, shubhasiz, dunyodagi eng buyuk joylardan biridir. Kanyondagi yovvoyi tabiat juda xilma-xil – u yerda maymunlar va antilopalar, shuningdek begemotlar va timsohlar kabi ba'zi xavfli hayvonlar bor.

VI

Dengiz bilan o'ralgan arka shaklidagi go'zal qoya hosilasi mavjud. Siz uning atrofidagi hududni qayiqda o'rganishingiz, iliq suvlarda suzishingiz yoki plyajda quyoshda toblanishingiz mumkin. Bu mukammal ta'til manzili qayerda bo'lishi mumkin?

Cathedral Cove, Yangi Zelandiya

Yangi Zelandiyadagi eng ko'p tashrif buyuriladigan sayyohlik joylaridan biri bo'lgan Koromandel yarim oroli 250 mildan (400 km) ortiq oq qumli plyajlarni taklif qiladi. Yarim orolni sayohat qilishning eng yaxshi usuli - kayakda; siz butun qirg'oq bo'ylab aylanishingiz yoki hatto sharsharadan pastga tushishingiz mumkin.

Hozirgi kunda Koromandelda ko'p odam yashamasa-da, u eng qadimgi Polineziya aholi punktlaridan biri deb hisoblanadi. Bu mashhur nafaqaga chiqish manzili – yarim oroldagi barcha aholining deyarli uchdan bir qismi 65 yosh va undan kattadir. U yerda joylashgan katta arka nomi bilan atalgan Cathedral Cove dengiz qo'riqxonasi bo'lib, unga faqat qayiqda yoki piyoda borish mumkin.
