Grammatika Qoidalari
Present Simple vs Present Continuous

O'yinlar va sport mavzusida bu zamonlardan ko'p foydalanamiz:

Present Simple - Odatiy harakatlar:

‚úì I play football every weekend.

‚úì She trains three times a week.

‚úì They support Manchester United.

Present Continuous - Hozirgi paytdagi harakatlar:

‚úì We are playing chess right now.

‚úì He is training for the competition.

‚úì The teams are competing today.

Gerund (-ing) after certain verbs

Ba'zi fe'llardan keyin -ing shakli ishlatiladi:

‚úì I enjoy playing tennis.

‚úì She finished watching the game.

‚úì They avoid losing matches.

‚úì We suggest practising more.

Used to - Oldingi odatlar

O'tmishda mavjud bo'lgan odatlarni ifodalash uchun:

‚úì I used to play football when I was young.

‚úì She used to be a professional swimmer.

‚úì We didn't use to watch sports on TV.
