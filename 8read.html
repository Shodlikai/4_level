Bitcoin: The Currency of the Future

Bitcoin is an alternative type of payment system. It is a type of internet or digital money, digital cash. It works just like an online wallet. You can use it to do business outside mainstream financial services. You can send money to your friends, buy stuff online and offline, or even invest in it, like you would do with stocks.

A Brief History of Bitcoin

Bitcoin is not an entirely new idea. Before Bitcoin, there were a few digital cash technologies available, but most of them needed more time to fully develop. In 2008, a paper about a peer-to-peer cash system, authored by Satoshi Nakamoto, was posted to a specialized mailing list. Some months later, in January 2009, the Bitcoin software was released. The very first supporters, who downloaded the software on the first day, received several bitcoins from the creator for free. In the first eight years of the operation, it is estimated that some 16.5 million Bitcoins were bought.

Why was it created?

Bitcoin was created as a response to the criticism suggesting that big banks are corrupt and not accountable, constantly misusing clients' money and charging enormous fees. The creator of Bitcoin invented something that eliminates the middleman – or, in this case, the bank – from financial exchanges. Because the Bitcoin system is decentralized, all transactions are very transparent, so there is no corruption. The clients always know what is going on and can control their own money. In brief, it is a virtual currency, and the users are free to choose among checks, wiring or cash when they want to make a transaction.

How Does Bitcoin Work?

A word often associated with Bitcoin is "blockchain", which is where all confirmed transactions Bitcoin users make are placed as "blocks". More specifically, whenever a "block", or a transaction, enters the online system, its validation is needed on the peer-to-peer computer network. That means that all Bitcoin users are aware of every single block, and this makes stealing or spending the same currency twice impossible. This is one of the reasons why millions of people trust Bitcoin and see a very bright future ahead of it. All transactions occur only between the buyer and the seller, with the help of private "keys", or passwords, they come up with, and their electronic gadgets.

How Can Bitcoin Be Bought?

At first, all new users must install a virtual wallet onto their electronic devices. This wallet is like an online bank account where all future transactions will be noted. Next step, Bitcoin needs some real money which will be translated into a virtual currency. This can be done through an online payment, a bank transfer or a third-party website. Once the funds are stored in the virtual wallet, the users should order bitcoins and then use them as they please. This simple process is very similar to trading stocks, for example.

Where Is Bitcoin Used?

Even though it is only several years old, Bitcoin has permeated to businesses and households around the world. Even billion-dollar businesses like PayPal and Microsoft allow their customers to use Bitcoin to pay for services. It is also possible to buy a necklace or a ring from the REED Jewelers jewelry chain in the United States using Bitcoin. In addition, people living in the countries with high levels of corruption find Bitcoin to be the easiest and most reliable channel for sending and receiving money.

Interesting Facts about Bitcoin

Rick Falkvinge, the Founder of the Swedish Pirate Party, said that "Bitcoin will do to banks what email did to the postal industry", meaning that banks will lose most of their business thanks to this new development. While Satoshi Nakamato is listed as the creator of Bitcoin, it is unknown whether this is a person or a group of people. The founder(s) claim to be Japanese; however, this is also not certain. If you lose your virtual wallet, you will lose your Bitcoins forever. It was reported that one Bitcoiner accidentally threw away his hard drive, losing 7,500 Bitcoins amounting to £4 million. Unlike in the offline world, refunds are not possible for Bitcoin transactions. One user learned this the hard way by accidentally sending $137,000 instead of $5 to a seller.

Bitcoin: Kelajak Valyutasi

Bitcoin — bu to'lov tizimining muqobil turi. Bu internet yoki raqamli pul, raqamli naqd pul turidir. U xuddi onlayn hamyon kabi ishlaydi. Siz undan asosiy moliyaviy xizmatlardan tashqarida biznes qilish uchun foydalanishingiz mumkin. Bitcoinning Qisqacha Tarixi

Bitcoin butunlay yangi g'oya emas. Bitcoindan oldin bir nechta raqamli naqd pul texnologiyalari mavjud edi, ammo ularning ko'pchiligi to'liq rivojlanish uchun ko'proq vaqt talab qilgan. 2008-yilda Satoshi Nakamoto muallifligidagi peer-to-peer (tengdoshga-tengdosh) pul tizimi haqidagi maqola maxsus pochta ro'yxatiga joylashtirildi. Bir necha oy o'tgach, 2009-yil yanvar oyida Bitcoin dasturiy ta'minoti chiqarildi. Birinchi kunda dasturiy ta'minotni yuklab olgan eng birinchi tarafdorlar yaratuvchidan bepul bir necha bitcoin oldilar. Faoliyatning dastlabki sakkiz yilida taxminan 16,5 million Bitcoin sotib olingan deb hisoblanadi.

U nima uchun yaratilgan?

Bitcoin katta banklarning korrupsiyalashgani va hisobdor emasligi, doimiy ravishda mijozlarning pullarini noto'g'ri ishlatishi va katta to'lovlar olishiga javob sifatida yaratilgan. Bitcoin yaratuvchisi moliyaviy almashinuvlardan vositachini – yoki bu holda, bankni – yo'q qiladigan narsani ixtiro qildi. Bitcoin tizimi markazlashmaganligi sababli, barcha tranzaksiyalar juda shaffof, shuning uchun korrupsiya yo'q. Mijozlar har doim nima bo'layotganini bilishadi va o'z pullarini nazorat qilishlari mumkin. Qisqacha aytganda, bu virtual valyuta va foydalanuvchilar tranzaksiya qilishni xohlaganlarida cheklar, o'tkazmalar yoki naqd pulni tanlashda erkindirlar.

Bitcoin Qanday Ishlaydi?

Bitcoin bilan tez-tez bog'liq bo'lgan so'z bu "blockchain" (blokcheyn), bu yerda Bitcoin foydalanuvchilari amalga oshiradigan barcha tasdiqlangan tranzaksiyalar "bloklar" sifatida joylashtiriladi. Aniqrog'i, qachonki "blok" yoki tranzaksiya onlayn tizimga kirsa, uning tasdig'i peer-to-peer kompyuter tarmog'ida talab qilinadi. Bu shuni anglatadiki, barcha Bitcoin foydalanuvchilari har bir blokdan xabardor bo'lishadi va bu bir xil valyutani ikki marta o'g'irlash yoki sarflashni imkonsiz qiladi. Bu millionlab odamlarning Bitcoinga ishonishining va uning oldida juda yorqin kelajakni ko'rishining sabablaridan biridir. Barcha tranzaksiyalar faqat xaridor va sotuvchi o'rtasida, ular o'ylab topgan shaxsiy "kalitlar" yoki parollar va ularning elektron gadjetlari yordamida amalga oshadi.

Bitcoinni Qanday Sotib Olish Mumkin?

Avvaliga, barcha yangi foydalanuvchilar o'z elektron qurilmalariga virtual hamyon o'rnatishlari kerak. Bu hamyon barcha kelajakdagi tranzaksiyalar qayd etiladigan onlayn bank hisob raqamiga o'xshaydi. Keyingi qadam, Bitcoin uchun virtual valyutaga aylantiriladigan haqiqiy pul kerak bo'ladi. Buni onlayn to'lov, bank o'tkazmasi yoki uchinchi tomon veb-sayti orqali amalga oshirish mumkin. Mablag'lar virtual hamyonda saqlangandan so'ng, foydalanuvchilar bitcoinlarga buyurtma berishlari va keyin ulardan xohlagancha foydalanishlari kerak. Bu oddiy jarayon, masalan, aksiyalar savdosiga juda o'xshaydi.

Bitcoin Qayerda Ishlatiladi?

Garchi u atigi bir necha yoshda bo'lsa-da, Bitcoin butun dunyo bo'ylab biznes va uy xo'jaliklariga kirib bordi. Hatto PayPal va Microsoft kabi milliard dollarlik bizneslar o'z mijozlariga xizmatlar uchun to'lov sifatida Bitcoindan foydalanishga ruxsat berishadi. Shuningdek, Qo'shma Shtatlardagi REED Jewelers zargarlik tarmog'idan Bitcoin yordamida marjon yoki uzuk sotib olish mumkin. Bundan tashqari, korrupsiya darajasi yuqori bo'lgan mamlakatlarda yashovchi odamlar Bitcoinni pul yuborish va qabul qilish uchun eng oson va ishonchli kanal deb bilishadi.
